ABOUT US

"The Goal Of A Triangle"

We are passionately dedicated to build the esports community and rely on a basic yet powerful philosophy - concentrating on fostering a positive culture that is crucial for team-based esports success. By offering advertising and promotional sponsorship, talent and team development, business management, coaching and support to professional and aspiring players who compete in esports, the Team Triangle organisation advances this philosophy in preceiving out goals.

"The Story Behind A Triangle"

Founded in January 2019, The Triangle Gaming Club, also known as "The Triangle" is an esports organization that is formed with a combination of three organizations. Timberwolf Basketball Asia, Triangle Esports and Aviant Art working together to address problems and achieve goals that seem to be out of reach when working alone. By combining the effort and expertise of different organisations, all partners in the network are able to innovate, grow and increase their competitiveness on many levels especially in esports.
